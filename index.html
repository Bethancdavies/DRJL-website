Jessica Livingston, PhD

Neuroscientist

Postdoctoral Fellow interested in studying brain repair strategies following stroke. Using both adult and neonatal stroke models, her research aims to improve recovery of motor and cognitive deficits using pharmacological and rehabilitative approaches. She is particularly interested in the role of endogenous post-injury neurogenesis, and is studying how we might better engage this natural repair strategy to improve and accelerate functional recovery.
